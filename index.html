What is CrowdMusic?

CrowdMusic is a small raising star in the world of musical crowdfunding. This project is created to make newcomer musicians from all over the world share their work, projects, ideas.

Feel free to share every your little musical idea, even if you think it's total garbage. Even in the most awful composition there are plenty of interesting ideas that can be improved with the help of other's advice. People may like your work, support you with some kind words and maybe even money!

The most special thing about CrowdMusic is that there are musicians in our team who can give some professional advice to their less experienced colleagues.
